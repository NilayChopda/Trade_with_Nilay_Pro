# 📱 How to Install Trade With Nilay Code

Your Pro Platform is ready to be installed as a native app!

## 📲 Step 1: Update Your Live App

Since we just added the **App Icons** and **Mobile Features**, you need to update the cloud version:

1. Open **GitHub Desktop**
2. You should see changes (icons + app.py)
3. Type summary: "Added mobile app support"
4. Click **"Commit to main"**
5. Click **"Push origin"**

## 📲 Step 2: Install on iPhone (iOS)

1. Open **Safari** (must be Safari)
2. Go to your app URL: `https://trade-with-nilay.streamlit.app`
3. Tap the **Share Details** button (square with arrow up) at bottom center
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. You'll see the "TN" logo!
6. Tap **Add**

## 📲 Step 3: Install on Android

1. Open **Chrome**
2. Go to your app URL: `https://trade-with-nilay.streamlit.app`
3. Tap the **Three Dots (⋮)** menu at top right
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** (or "Install App")
5. Tap **Add** automatically

## 🚀 The Native App Experience

Now verify it works:
1. Go to your phone's home screen
2. Find the **"Trade With Nilay"** app icon
3. Tap it
4. It should open **full screen** (no browser bar)
5. It looks and feels just like a real app!

---

**Need Help?**
If the icon doesn't appear immediately, clear your browser cache or wait 5 minutes for the update to propagate.
