## 🎯 What's New (Phase 4)

### ✅ EOD Analytics Engine (`backend/services/eod_analytics.py`)

A powerful analytics service that runs daily at **3:35 PM** (market close) to generate insights.

**Features:**
1. **Market Breadth**: Calculates Advances vs Declines ratio to gauge market sentiment.
2. **Top Movers**: Identifies Top 5 Gainers and Losers from the 2000+ stock universe.
3. **Telegram Reporting**: Automatically formats and sends a beautiful summary to your channel.
4. **Reliability**: Updated Telegram service to use synchronous requests for maximum stability.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

### Automatic Mode
The system handles everything! Just keep `run_scheduler.bat` running.
- **3:35 PM**: EOD Report is generated and sent to Telegram.

### Manual Mode (Testing)
You can generate the report ON DEMAND at any time.

1. Go to `G:\My Drive\Trade_with_Nilay`
2. Double-click **`run_eod_test.bat`**
3. Check your Telegram! 📱
